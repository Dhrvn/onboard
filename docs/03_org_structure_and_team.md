## Team Norms

- **Slack is async by default.** Don't expect instant replies outside core hours (11am–4pm IST)
- **@here and @channel** are used sparingly — only for urgent, time-sensitive things
- **DMs are fine** but if the answer might help others, post in the relevant channel instead
- **Video calls:** Camera on is encouraged but never mandatory
- **English is the working language** in all written communication (docs, Slack, PRs). Spoken 1:1s can be in any language

## New Joiner Buddies Program

Every new joiner is assigned a **buddy** — a peer (not your manager) who you can ask anything without feeling judged. Your buddy is assigned by HR before Day 1 and will reach out to you on Slack.

Buddies are available for:
- "Dumb questions" you're embarrassed to ask your manager
- Navigating the social dynamics of the team
- Recommendations for lunch spots near the office
- Honest feedback on how you're coming across in the first few weeks

If you haven't heard from your buddy by noon on Day 1, ping @ananya.iyer.
