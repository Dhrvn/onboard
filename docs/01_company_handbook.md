# Nexus Labs — Company Handbook
*Last updated: April 2026*

---

## Who We Are

Nexus Labs is a fintech startup building real-time credit risk infrastructure for emerging markets. We help banks and lenders in Southeast Asia and India make faster, fairer lending decisions using alternative data — mobile behavior, utility payments, and transaction patterns — instead of traditional credit scores.

Founded in 2021, we are 54 people across Mumbai, Bangalore, and Singapore. We have raised $18M Series A (led by Sequoia India) and are processing over 2 million credit decisions per month.

---

## Our Mission

To make credit accessible to the 1.4 billion people in Asia who are "credit invisible" — they exist, they earn, they pay bills, but banks can't see them.

---

## Values

**1. Speed with judgment** — We move fast but we don't cut corners on data quality or compliance. If something feels off, slow down and flag it.

**2. Radical transparency** — Bad news travels fast here. We'd rather know about a problem on Day 1 than Day 10. No hiding mistakes.

**3. Customer obsession** — Our customers are banks and lenders, but our end users are the borrowers. Always keep the borrower in mind.

**4. Own it fully** — If you picked it up, you own it end to end. No "that's not my job."

**5. Learn in public** — Share what you're learning, including failures. Weekly #learnings channel on Slack.
